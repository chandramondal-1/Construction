Crescent Niwas

Current residential development at South Bagha Jatin Colony, Pradhan Nagar, Siliguri.

Project Overview

Where quality, comfort and planning come together.

Crescent Niwas is designed as an 8-flat apartment project with a mixed configuration of 1BHK, 2BHK and 3BHK homes. The brochure positions the project around comfort, connectivity, vastu-oriented planning and premium quality materials.
