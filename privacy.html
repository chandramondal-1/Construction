Privacy Policy

This draft privacy page is included to strengthen trust and future deployment readiness.

Information you share

When you use contact or site visit forms, you may share your name, phone number, email address and project details. That information is used only for follow-up communication related to your enquiry.

How enquiries are handled

This current build routes form details toward email-based follow-up. On future deployment, the site can be connected to a CRM, inbox workflow or secured backend form endpoint.
